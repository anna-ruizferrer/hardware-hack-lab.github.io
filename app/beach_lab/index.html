Beach Lab

Beach Lab is a series of projects done by people at Thoughtworks who have some spare time between client assignments. Participants are invited to a brainstorming session to kick off the process, then one idea from that brainstorming process is chosen and developed over the next few weeks.

If you'd like to get involved, contact Greg Dutcher.
