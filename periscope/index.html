Periscope into the lab

Sometimes we broadcast live events, presentations and so on via Periscope.

How to join

Periscope announces broadcasts via twitter. Keep an eye on the twitter feed on this page for a link to the latest broadcast. Note that older broadcasts are archived - check for today's date.

If you don't see a link to the broadcast yet, just keep an eye on it. The list refreshes every 30 seconds so you won't miss it when it comes!
