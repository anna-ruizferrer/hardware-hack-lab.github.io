Periscope into the lab

Sometimes we broadcast live events, presentations and so on via Periscope.

How to join

Periscope announces broadcasts via twitter. Keep an eye on the twitter feed here for a link to the latest broadcast. Note that older broadcasts are archived - check the tweet times.

If you don't see a link to the right broadcast yet, just keep an eye on it. The list refreshes every 30 seconds so you won't miss it when it comes!

Chat in realtime

To chat with us and other viewers in realtime, launch the broadcast link from the tweet with the Periscope app on your phone. Download the app here:
